About

What is this?

French–English Dictionary is a free, ad-supported web app for looking up French words. It is an independent project with no affiliation to any organisation.

Data source

All dictionary content is fetched in real time from English Wiktionary via its public API, and is used under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-SA 4.0) licence.

Features

  • Definitions with usage labels moved to end — e.g. "to eat (transitive)"
  • Etymology
  • Verb conjugation and noun inflection tables
  • Synonyms, antonyms, derived and related terms
  • Example sentences with English translations
  • Clickable internal links — look up any linked French word instantly
  • Installable as a PWA (Add to Home Screen on mobile or desktop)

Advertising

This site displays ads via Google AdSense to cover hosting and development costs. We do not sell or share user data ourselves. See our Privacy Policy for details.
