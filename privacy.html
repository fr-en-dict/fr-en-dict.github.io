Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how French–English Dictionary ("the site", "we") handles information when you use this website. This is an independent project operated anonymously.

1. Information we collect

We do not directly collect or store any personal information. No account registration is required or offered.

We store one item in your browser's localStorage to remember that you have acknowledged this cookie notice. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.

2. Google AdSense

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your browsing history across websites.

  • Opt out of personalised ads: Google Ads Settings
  • General opt-out (US): aboutads.info
  • General opt-out (EU): youronlinechoices.eu

3. Third-party services

  • Wiktionary API — your search terms are sent directly from your browser to the Wiktionary public API. We do not intercept or log them. See the Wikimedia Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP address. See Google Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

We do not set tracking cookies. Third-party services listed above (Google AdSense, Google Fonts) may set their own cookies governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Your rights

If you are in the EEA, UK, or California, you may have rights regarding personal data held by third parties. Please contact those services directly. As this site operates anonymously and holds no personal data, we are unable to respond to individual data requests.

6. Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes.
